Write a LinkedIn post in the user's brand voice.

## Step 1: Load context
Read context/brand-voice.md and context/target-audience.md.

## Step 2: Clarify if needed
If not specified, ask: "Is this for the company page or your personal profile?"
If the topic is too vague to write anything, ask one clarifying question. Otherwise proceed.

## Step 3: Write the post

Rules:
- Opening line: a strong hook -- a bold claim, a specific observation, or a direct question. Never start with "I'm excited to share", "Thrilled to announce", or "Pleased to".
- Body: 3-5 short paragraphs or bullet points. One idea per line. White space matters on LinkedIn.
- Close: a question that invites comments, or a soft single CTA
- Length: 150-300 words. Do not pad.
- Hashtags: 2-3 at the end only
- UK English, no em dashes
- Reflect the tone from brand-voice.md
- Frame the message for the audience in target-audience.md

## Step 4: Save the draft
Save to projects/linkedin/YYYY-MM-DD-[topic-slug].md with this header:
```
Post type: [Company page / Personal]
Status: Draft
Date: YYYY-MM-DD
```

## Step 5: Present and confirm
Show the full post. Ask: "Happy with this, or any changes?"
