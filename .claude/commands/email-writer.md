Draft a marketing or outreach email in the user's brand voice.

## Step 1: Load context
Read context/brand-voice.md and context/target-audience.md.

## Step 2: Identify the email type
- Marketing email (newsletter, nurture, announcement): value-first, one message, single CTA
- Outreach email (sent to a specific person): personalised hook, relevant pain point, low-friction CTA

If the type is not clear, ask before writing.

## Step 3: Write the email

Rules:
- Subject line: under 50 characters, specific rather than clever
- Preview text: one sentence that builds on the subject line
- Body: short paragraphs, 2-3 sentences each, one idea per paragraph
- CTA: one clear action only
- UK English, no em dashes
- Reflect tone from brand-voice.md
- Frame around what matters to the audience in target-audience.md

## Step 4: Save the draft
Save to projects/email/drafts/YYYY-MM-DD-[topic-slug].md structured as:
```
Subject: [subject line]
Preview: [preview text]

---

[Full email body]
```

## Step 5: Present and confirm
Show the full email. Ask: "Ready to use, or anything to change?"
